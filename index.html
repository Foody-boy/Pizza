1. Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a creamy and flavorful North Indian dish loved around the world.

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken
  • 1 cup curd
  • 2 onions
  • 3 tomatoes
  • Butter and cream
  • Spices and salt

Recipe

  1. Marinate chicken with curd and spices.
  2. Cook onions and tomato puree in butter.
  3. Add cooked chicken to the gravy.
  4. Pour cream and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan.

2. Paneer Butter Masala

A rich vegetarian curry made with paneer cubes and creamy tomato gravy.

Ingredients

  • 250 g paneer
  • Tomatoes and onions
  • Butter and cream
  • Indian spices

Recipe

  1. Cook onion and tomato gravy.
  2. Add spices and cream.
  3. Mix paneer cubes carefully.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.

3. Masala Dosa

A crispy South Indian dosa filled with spicy potato stuffing.

Ingredients

  • Rice batter
  • Urad dal
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onions and spices

Recipe

  1. Prepare dosa batter and ferment it.
  2. Cook spicy potato filling.
  3. Spread batter on tawa.
  4. Add filling and fold dosa.
  5. Serve with chutney and sambar.

4. Chole Bhature

A famous Punjabi dish with spicy chickpeas and fluffy fried bread.

Recipe

  1. Cook chickpeas with masala gravy.
  2. Prepare bhature dough.
  3. Deep fry bhature until puffed.
  4. Serve hot with onions and pickle.

5. Hyderabadi Biryani

A royal rice dish cooked with spices and chicken.

Recipe

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and curd.
  2. Cook basmati rice separately.
  3. Layer rice and chicken in a pot.
  4. Add saffron milk and fried onions.
  5. Cook on dum for 30 minutes.

6. Rajma Chawal

Kidney beans curry served with steamed rice.

Recipe

  1. Pressure cook rajma.
  2. Prepare onion tomato gravy.
  3. Add rajma and simmer.
  4. Serve with rice.

7. Pav Bhaji

A popular Mumbai street food made with mashed vegetables and buttered pav.

Recipe

  1. Boil and mash vegetables.
  2. Cook with spices and butter.
  3. Toast pav buns with butter.
  4. Serve hot with lemon and onions.

8. Palak Paneer

Healthy spinach gravy cooked with paneer cubes.

Recipe

  1. Boil and blend spinach.
  2. Cook onion tomato masala.
  3. Add spinach puree and paneer.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes and serve.

9. Dal Tadka

Simple lentil curry topped with spicy tempering.

Recipe

  1. Pressure cook dal.
  2. Prepare tadka with garlic and spices.
  3. Mix with dal and simmer.
  4. Serve hot with rice.

10. Gulab Jamun

A sweet Indian dessert soaked in sugar syrup.

Recipe

  1. Prepare dough using khoya and flour.
  2. Make small balls and fry them.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup.
  4. Soak gulab jamuns in syrup.
  5. Serve warm or chilled.
