🍕 Homemade Pizza Recipe

Make a delicious homemade pizza with cheesy toppings, crispy crust, and fresh ingredients right at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables of your choice
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough.
  2. Spread pizza sauce evenly.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
